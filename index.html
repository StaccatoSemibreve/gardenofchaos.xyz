The Garden of Chaos

A low-stakes weekly Fantasy Strike tournament based around following silly rulesets at every step, run by Janet (you know, from the Fantasy Strike Discord server) with logos and general design by Daniel Stretulch (who is awesome btw)! If you wish to participate or watch, please use the bracket or stream links respectively from the navigation bar. You can also find a list of upcoming Fantasy Strike events in the Events page.

This week's tournament format is: Oops, All Vals! (a variant of Teams)

Single battle, best of 3, no counterpicks. All players are grouped into two teams, where they must each play from a given set of characters (all of whom are Valerie), chosen on a per-tournament basis. The teams play sequential matches against each other according to a custom team battle manager until one team wins.

It will be run on Thursday the 25th of June at 21:00 BST (20:00 UTC, 13:00 PDT, 16:00 EDT). This week it will yet again be commentated by Janet and Daniel! ^_^

A randomly selected participant will receive a steam key for Kerbal Space Program, courtesy of ErickDRedd!
